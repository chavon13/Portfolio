Who I am

Web developer based out of Covington

I am Ashley Savage. I am a passionate, patient web developer with an intrinsic love for learning. I love creating websites from scratch and take a lot of pride in my work. I've been working with computer since I was about 7, taking computers apart and putting them back together. In my teen years, I had a small business removing viruses from computers. I also work very well in creative spaces and enjoy a lot of creativity in my free time including painting and writing.

Working in call centers for over ten years taught me a lot about people and meeting their needs. It also taught me a lot about patience, problem-solving and service with a smile. Without my call center work, I don't think I would be the developer that I am today. Coding is such rewarding work in that you get build things from scratch and then see it come to life! I enjoy working with HTML and CSS as well as Javascript.
